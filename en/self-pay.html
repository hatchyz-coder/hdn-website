From idea to a working clinical service.

We do not stop at proposing a menu. We design the patient journey and the internal workflow needed to operate it.

Service design

Define the offer, target patient, delivery model, required partners, and operating conditions.

Pricing and economics

Review price, gross margin, cost structure, repeat design, and realistic sales targets.

Patient communication

Create clear explanations, FAQs, website content, LINE messaging, and consent-related guidance.

Operational workflow

Design staff roles, booking, questionnaires, payment, delivery, follow-up, and escalation routes.

Promotion

Plan website, landing page, social, LINE, referral, and local marketing around actual capacity.

Compliance review

Identify risk in claims, pricing communication, medical advertising, and promotional wording.
