LHub for Clinics

Connect booking, questionnaires, payments, and follow-up through LINE.

LHub helps clinics turn a LINE Official Account into a practical patient engagement workflow. It brings booking, pre-visit forms, payment guidance, patient segmentation, messaging, product sales, and follow-up into one operating environment.
